Welcome to Our School

Rayapur Sayed Abdul Latif Secondary School is one of the most prestigious educational institutions in Nalchity, Jhalokathi. Established in 1950, our school has been providing quality education to students for over 70 years.

Our school campus is spread over 5 acres of land, providing a serene and conducive environment for learning. The campus includes well-equipped classrooms, a library, science labs, and sports facilities.

We have a highly qualified and dedicated team of teachers who are committed to nurturing the potential of every student. Our curriculum is designed to foster intellectual growth, creativity, and critical thinking.

Our school also emphasizes extracurricular activities, including sports, music, art, and drama, to ensure the holistic development of our students.

We take pride in our alumni, who have excelled in various fields, including education, business, and public service. Our school continues to strive for excellence in all areas.

National Flag of Bangladesh

The national flag of Bangladesh consists of a red disc on a green field. The red disc represents the sun rising over Bengal, and the green field symbolizes the lushness of the land of Bangladesh. The flag was adopted on 17th January 1972.
